# PantryMate

### AI-Powered Recipe Generator & Weekly Meal Planner

An intelligent web application that transforms your available ingredients into delicious recipes using Google Gemini AI, with personalized weekly meal planning and nutritional tracking.

---

## 📌 Overview

**PantryMate** is a modern AI-powered platform designed to:

- Help home cooks create delicious meals from ingredients they already have
- Reduce food waste by optimizing ingredient usage
- Provide personalized weekly meal plans aligned with nutritional goals
- Demonstrate AI integration in consumer applications

This project combines artificial intelligence, modern web technologies, and user-centered design to solve everyday cooking challenges.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🥗 AI Recipe Generator
- **Ingredient-Based Search**: Enter ingredients from your fridge or pantry
- **Cuisine Selection**: Choose from Italian, Mexican, Asian, American, Mediterranean
- **Meal Type Filtering**: Filter by Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack, Beverage
- **Complete Recipes**: Detailed instructions, cooking times, and ingredient lists
- **Nutritional Information**: Estimated calories, protein, carbs, and fats

### 📅 Weekly Meal Planner
- **Nutritional Goals**: Set daily calorie and macronutrient targets
- **Personalized Plans**: 7-day meal plans based on your pantry
- **Meal Swapping**: Easily replace meals with alternatives
- **Drag & Drop**: Rearrange your week intuitively
- **Print Ready**: Generate printable meal plans for your fridge

### 🧠 Powered by AI
- Google Gemini API generates creative, unique recipes
- Smart ingredient optimization reduces food waste
- Dietary templates: Keto, Balanced, High-Protein presets

### 📱 Responsive Design
- Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
- Touch-friendly interface for mobile users
- Print-optimized layouts

---

## 🧠 Concept

This project solves a universal problem: **"What can I cook with what I have?"**

### The Problem
- Millions of people waste food because they don't know what to make
- Meal planning is time-consuming and stressful
- Dietary goals are hard to track without proper tools
- Recipe apps often require specific ingredients you don't have

### The Solution
**PantryMate** uses AI to:
1. Analyze your available ingredients
2. Generate creative, practical recipes
3. Help you plan meals that meet your health goals
4. Reduce food waste by using what you already have

It demonstrates both:
- **Technical AI integration skills**
- **Product thinking & real-world problem solving**

## 🧩 Website Structure

### Landing Page
- **Hero Section**: Tagline and call-to-action
- **Problem Statement**: Why PantryMate exists
- **Solution Overview**: How AI helps you cook
- **How It Works**: 3-step workflow (Add → Generate → Plan)
- **Features Showcase**: Recipe generator and meal planner
- **Portfolio Cards**: Sample recipe previews
- **Call-To-Action**: Start cooking

### Recipe Generator Page
- Ingredient input with autocomplete
- Visual pantry tags with remove functionality
- Cuisine and meal type filters
- Recipe card grid with expandable details
- Nutritional information panel

### Meal Planner Page
- Nutritional goal setting (calories, macros)
- Dietary template presets (Keto, Balanced, High-Protein)
- 7-day calendar view with meals
- Daily nutritional totals vs. goals
- Meal swapping and drag-and-drop reordering
- Print functionality

### Project Detail Pages
- Full recipe previews
- Step-by-step cooking instructions
- Ingredient breakdown with quantities
- Nutritional breakdown
- AI-generated food description
